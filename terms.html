All lesson content in Lingvoo is original content created by the Lingvoo development team. Our educational methodology and content structure are informed by the following widely recognized standards and resources:

  • CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages): Our proficiency levels (A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2) follow the CEFR framework, which is the international standard for describing language ability. Lesson difficulty and vocabulary targets are aligned with CEFR level descriptors.
